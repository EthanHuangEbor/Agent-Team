# Output Contract

The authoritative output rules live in `skills/sansheng-liubu/references/output-contract.md`.

Every final memorial must include:

- task title and goal;
- Zhongshu plan;
- Menxia decision with feasibility, completeness, risk, and resources;
- Shangshu dispatch summary;
- ministry results or explicit non-applicability;
- self-review and repair rounds, capped at two;
- validation evidence actually used;
- remaining risks and next steps.

Do not use role names as proof of work. Each role needs evidence, a decision, or a skip reason.
