Keep it compact. For small tasks, merge sections while preserving the required facts.

## Evidence Rules

- Name files, commands, citations, screenshots, or reasoning checks actually used.
- Say "not run" when validation was not possible.
- Mark a ministry as "not applicable" rather than inventing work.
- Do not present role labels as proof of work; each role needs a concrete contribution or explicit skip reason.
